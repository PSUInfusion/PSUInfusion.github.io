Lineup by Infusion

ASU Andaaz:

Andaaz is ASU’s one and only competitive Bollywood fusion dance team. Andaaz was formed just over a year ago and this is our first time competing at Infusion. The passion for dance has brought us together and we aim to spread the South Asian culture through the joy of dance. We are excited to experience the Happy Valley for the first time! The word "Andaaz" means style and we hope to show our Andaaz on the Infusion stage.

Broad Street Baadshahz:

The BROAD STREET BAADSHAHZ are an all male award winning South Asian Fusion Dance Team, located in the city of brotherly love, Philadelphia, PA. The Baadshahz are the #1 fusion team in the nation. Dancers have joined us from all over the Northeast specializing in: Bollywood, Bhangra, Hip Hop, and Martial Arts. Comprising of different races, ethnic groups, and backgrounds we consider ourselves a melting pot that share a common goal in pursuing the passion of dance. By meshing different styles of dance together we strive to provide our audiences with not one, but numerous breath-taking stunts, formations, and sequences of choreography.

EuphorEk Ada:

EuphorEk Ada is New York City’s all female fusion dance team. They strive to break the common perception of the "typical" female dance team by implementing stunts, gimmicks, and unique choreography into their performances. They aspire to compete and entertain at events throughout the nation; and pride ourselves by being able to express their dedication, passion, and teamwork through dance. Their individual style's differ, but somehow translate into one, Ek Ada.

Boston University Jalwa

We are Boston University Jalwa, BU’s award-winning co-ed fusion team established in 2004, making this year our 10-year anniversary. As a fusion team, we combine all sorts of dance including bollywood, hip-hop, bhangra, classical, and contemporary styles of dance into one performance. We have been to Bollywood America twice in the past four years and have represented Boston University with multiple placings at several competitions. We are not just a dance team; we are a family. Shhh Jalwa's comin'!.

Delaware Kamaal

Delaware Kamaal is the University of Delaware’s official and ONLY fusion- Bollywood dance team. We come from all over the country, and are always amped up with Blue Hen pride. Delaware may be the 2nd smallest state in the US, but it doesn’t stop our big performances. We incorporate all different types of dance styles, and have one of the most diverse teams, which we are so proud of. We’re known for our quirky and funny routines, so you’re in for a surprise this year. Are you ready?

Penn Masti

What's life without a little Masti? Bringing high energy and a little mischief to each performance, Penn Masti adds a co-ed twist to South Asian fusion dance. We pride ourselves on experimenting with various dance styles: Hindi film dance, bhangra, hip-hop, jazz, raas, bharatanatyam, lyrical, modern, folk, and even broadway. But more importantly, we're all about fun and family. Good times are what we believe in, and dance is only one of the many ways we like to live it up.

Rutger's SAPA

SAPA's dance troupe made history in 2000 by becoming the first co-ed South Asian dance troupe in the United States. Incorporating various elements of dancing, SAPA integrated styles such as Classical, Bollywood, and Hip-hop to introduce the Rutgers community to a style of dance that was never before seen on campus and soon after, the entire nation. The members of SAPA wish to enhance the involvement of the South Asian community at Rutgers through this expressive form and to showcase the art of Indian dancing from its roots to the evolving, energetic style that it is today.

Spartan Zariya:

Spartan Zaariya is MSU's very first filmi fusion team! We formed in Spring 2014, when two of MSU RaaSparty’s former captains decided to branch out and collaborate with a few girls to start a fusion team. Spartan Zaariya’s board is experienced in many dance styles, including kathak, garba, bhangra, raas, bharatanatyam, hip hop, etc. We want to use our versatility to create neverbefore-seen concepts within our routine. We strive to bring joy to our audience and the circuit through dance and our unique style. GO GREEN!

UC Dhadak

UC Dhadak is the University of Cincinnati’s premiere Bollywood Fusion Dance Team. They’re a diverse group of students who simply LOVE to dance. For the past six years UC Dhadak has been competing in the nationally competitive Bollywood Fusion Desi Dance circuit. Looking forward to the 2015 dance season, UC Dhadak is excited to take the Penn State Infusion stage for the first time!
