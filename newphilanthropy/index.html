ABOUT THON
The Penn State IFC/Panhellenic Dance MaraTHON is an annual, year-round fundraising event for The Four Diamonds Fund, at the Penn State Children’s Hospital in Hershey, PA. Over the past 40 years, the students of Penn State have raised over $80 million and continue to lend support to thousands of children and families fighting pediatric cancer. The year culminates in a no-sitting, no-sleeping, 46-hour dance maraTHON held every February.

CONTRIBUTIONS
Over the years, THON has raised more than $136 million to support families in paying for pediatric cancer treatment and facilitate research. This collective effort, involving more than 16,000 students, has become one of Penn State’s greatest and most unifying traditions. As a percentage of Infusion's proceeds goes towards THON, your sponsorship will benefit a great cause.

ABOUT ACLU
Building on our foundation of philanthropy, the American Civil Liberties Union (ACLU) is a nonprofit organization that focuses on defending and preserving the individual rights and liberties that the Constitution and the laws of the United States guarantee everyone in this country. It was founded in 1920 and has been working to solve civil liberties cases in courts, legislatures, and communities in our nation.

CONTRIBUTIONS
With more than 4 million members, activists, and supporters, the ACLU is a nationwide organization that fights tirelessly in all 50 states, Puerto Rico, and Washington, D.C., to safeguard everyone’s rights. As a percentage of Infusion’s proceeds goes towards this foundation, your sponsorship could benefit a great cause.
