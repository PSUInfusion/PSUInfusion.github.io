Infusion Rewind



Infusion Rewind will be held virtually on Saturday, March 20th, 2021. We will initially conduct this competition through a social media and fan-based voting process. All 22 teams who have placed at a 1st, 2nd, or 3rd place standing at Infusion (2013-2020) will be included in this voting process, if they choose to participate. People will be able to vote for their favorite placing teams from each year. After voting, we will have our lineup of 8 performances for Infusion Rewind. The official winner of Infusion Rewind will be decided through voting on the livestream and will only be available to those who attend the livestream, with the final winner announced at the end.
