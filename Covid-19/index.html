COVID-19 UPDATE

After careful consideration and thought surrounding the COVID-19 pandemic, we regret to announce that we have decided to cancel Infusion 2021. While this was not an easy decision to come to, the Infusion 2021 Board realizes that this is in the best interest of our board, committee, performers and audience members. COVID-19 has impacted many lives around the world and our frontline workers are still fighting hard to keep it under control. Infusion advocates for prioritizing health and safety, and we do not encourage any team to break social distancing guidelines. Although a conventional competition is not possible for this season, we still plan on bringing the heat to our community this Spring. Check out our plans for a virtual show in the video below!
